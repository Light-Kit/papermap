Imaging is mostly the wet-lab and image-text infrastructure that pathology and spatial FMs build on. STAMP-Cell (2025) turns Xenium/CosMx/MERSCOPE/PhenoCycler into scalable single-cell profilers — the wet-lab counterpart to virtual spatial proteomics. BiomedCLIP is the biomedical image-text contrastive model on large-scale figure-caption pairs — the broad foundation for multimodal medical AI. Datasets: HuBMAP is the human-tissue spatial reference atlas; HEST-1k is the histology-with-spatial-transcriptomics benchmark dataset coupling imaging and gene expression. Recursion is the AI-native biotech using FMs (especially image-FM) for drug discovery — the commercial anchor for image-FM-driven phenotypic screening. The pattern: imaging is increasingly the substrate other FMs run on top of, not a standalone modality.
