4D modelling adds the time axis to whole-cell simulation — the explicit mech-modelling-meets-AIVC bridge. UIUC 4DWCM is the 4D whole-cell model that mixes mechanistic equations with learned components — the paper that operationalises the bridge. CompuCell3D is the mechanistic-multicellular community's annual workshop and codebase — the parallel-universe to AIVC where simulators are written rather than learned. The pattern: 4D modelling is one of the answers to the "more data alone won't fix it" verdict from the causal-transportability framing — if you can write the right time-resolved dynamics by hand for the parts you know, you only need data for the parts you don't. Tightly linked to the mech-modeling, lineage, and digital-twin topics. Small now; likely grows as the post-reckoning response moves past static cell representations.
