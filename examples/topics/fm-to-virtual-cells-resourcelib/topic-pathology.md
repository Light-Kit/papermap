Pathology is the family where biology FMs first cleared peer-reviewed clinical thresholds. The 2025 anchor models — UNI2-h (681M, HF-gated since Jan 2025), Virchow / Virchow-2 (1.85B current ceiling), CHIEF — set the encoder layer. PathChat-2 (>1M instruction pairs) is the successor assistant; PathChat-DX is the first FDA-Breakthrough generative-AI pathology tool. Owkin Pathology Explorer wraps pathology FMs in a clinical agent inside Claude. 2026 brings GPFM (large-scale gigapixel), PathPT, mStar (ST + WSI fusion), PathOrchestra (ensemble). Hex-Nat Med 2026 ports the family to spatial proteomics with strong clinical numbers. Janowczyk-UNI-EGFR (197 patients, 43 % testing avoidance) is the first deployment-grade clinical FM paper. AstraZeneca's Modella acquisition (JPM 2026) signals big-pharma consolidation has begun.
