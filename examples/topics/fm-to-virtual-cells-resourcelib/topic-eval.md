The evaluation thread is the most-published lane of 2025–2026. Ahlmann-Eltze (Nat Meth Aug 2025) is the canonical reckoning paper; Wenkel proposed latent-additive + scGPT-embeddings as the new floor; Wu (Nat Meth Jan 2026) decomposed failure axis-by-axis across 27 methods × 29 datasets × 6 metrics. Liu sc-Eval (Adv Sci) is the strongest "is the paradigm worth it" framing. Cell-dynamics-zero-shot extends the critique to RNA-velocity. CellBench-LS shows FMs lead cell-type ID but classical methods win gene-expression. Han RWD adds industry-grade robustness gaps. Montazeri 2026 opens a second front: Wasserstein/Energy distances mislead in high-dim gene space. SIS (Microsoft) is the metric for whether multimodal FMs extract genuinely cross-modal information. The contrarian (Feb 2026) keeps the verdict provisional.
