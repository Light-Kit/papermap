Perturb-seq is the substrate underneath everything in the perturbation-prediction reckoning. The canonical test sets: Replogle 2022, Norman 2019. The 2025 inflection: Tahoe-100M (first large-scale perturbation atlas framed as virtual-cell training data, from Vevo + Arc + CZ Biohub). Tahoe-Arc-CZBiohub (2026) is the partnership announcement — the big three of US AIVC join up around the dataset. Pertpy (Nat Methods 2025) is the scverse-blessed analysis toolbox: distances, perturbation spaces, harmonisation. PerturbFate (2026) maps convergent regulators of melanoma drug resistance — the disease-translation arm. PerturbHD (Stanford), Flex v2 (Broad), ProPer-seq are next-generation platforms — currently mostly names. Patrick Hsu (Evo2 + STATE co-PI, Tahoe-100M architect) is the centre of gravity.
