Westlake's bid for a proteomics FM and the headline result so far. AIPC-Westlake is the Westlake-run AI-proteomics challenge, bridging AIVC and mass-spec ML; it's the community on-ramp for new labs into the area. sc-pSILAC is the single-cell pSILAC variant (Zilu Ye attribution from talk notes; not yet citable but worth tracking — the wet-lab assay that would feed single-cell proteomics FMs). The pattern: the mass-spec FM thread is small enough that all three items chain back to Westlake / Tiannan Guo's group. Wider mass-spec ML is much larger; only the FM-flavoured corner shows up here.
