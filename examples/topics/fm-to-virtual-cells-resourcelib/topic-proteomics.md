Proteomics is the modality where Chinese labs are most visible at the FM frontier. DIA-BERT (Nat Comm 2025, Westlake) — transformer pre-trained on 276M peptide precursors, +51 % protein IDs vs DIA-NN. scTranslator (Nat BME 2025, Tencent AI Lab) — align-free scRNA→protein generation, 2M cells + 18k bulk samples. ProtiCelli (Lundberg lab) is the proteomics-anchored virtual-cell project — mostly slide-trail today. AIPC-Westlake is the AI-proteomics challenge bridging AIVC and mass-spec ML; Westlake's annual Tiannan-Guo AIVC symposium is the convening event. The cross-cut to clinical: Hex-Nat Med 2026 (H&E → 40-protein spatial proteomics on 2298 NSCLC patients) is the proteomics-FM result with the strongest clinical numbers. sc-pSILAC is the single-cell proteomics assay variant — Zilu Ye attribution from talk notes, not yet citable but worth tracking.
