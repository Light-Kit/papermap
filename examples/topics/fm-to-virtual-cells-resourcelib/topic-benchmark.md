Benchmarks are where the reckoning landed and where the response is judged. Single-cell: CellBench-LS (2026, FMs lead cell-type ID, classical methods win gene-expression), Open Problems v2 (community sc benchmark suite), BioLLM (unified APIs over scBERT/Geneformer/scGPT/scFoundation for fair comparison). Pathology: Campanella 2025 (multi-task pathology benchmark). Virtual cell: Virtual Cell Challenge (Arc 2025, 5000+ teams; most submissions scored worse than mean baseline on MAE, winning approach was hybrid using protein embeddings + classical features). Perturbation prediction: Perturbation-prediction-vs-baselines (Nat Methods 2025, linear models match or beat scGPT and GEARS). VCBench (arXiv) is a named virtual-cell benchmark whose details are still being chased. The story arc: benchmarks led the reckoning by exposing leaderboard inflation; they now anchor the response.
