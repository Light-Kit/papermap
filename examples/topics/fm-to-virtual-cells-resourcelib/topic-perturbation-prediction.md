Perturbation prediction is where the sc-FM reckoning landed first, and where the response wave is densest. The new generation: xVERSE (non-LM architecture, +17.9 % over leading sc-FMs), TxPert (multi-knowledge-graph priors — Wenkel co-authored both critique and response), MAP-Perturb (zero-shot for unprofiled drugs), sc-FM-perturb-adapter (<1 % trainable params on a frozen FM, generalises where CPA/GEARS/scGen don't). Diffusion alternatives: x-Cell (Wang 2026), Squidiff. Optimal transport: scale-conditional-transport (atlas-scale endpoint framing). RL: CellFluxRL (Stanford/CZI/Lundberg axis). Commercial validation: Turbine raised $25M. The contrarian (Feb 2026) argues scale fixes it. The 2026 Echoes Challenge (Myllia) keeps the benchmark cycle moving.
