Lineage is a small but precise thread combining perturbation screening with developmental tracing. PerturbFate (2026) maps convergent regulators of melanoma drug resistance — perturbation screening followed across fate trajectories rather than steady-state expression. Cao virtual-embryos (2026) is the developmental extension: integrating single-cell and spatial data into a developmental simulator that tracks cells along lineage branches. The pattern: lineage is the temporal dimension perturbation-prediction has been missing — Wu (Nat Methods 2026) showed most sc-FM evaluations are steady-state, and the rare-cohort/time-resolved-perturbation axes in the uncovered-evaluation table are exactly the holes lineage work could fill. Adjacent to the perturb-seq, perturbation-prediction, and 4d-modeling topics — currently only two items but a likely growth area in 2026–2027.
