Interpretability is one of two waves explaining the sc-FM reckoning at the mechanism level (the other is causal). The SAE wave: Adams 2025 (protein FMs, started the biology-FM interpretability wave); Hibou-LP-SAE 2024 (first pathology-FM SAE); SAE-scGPT 2025 (independent confirmation on scGPT); Simon & Zou 2026 (Geneformer + scGPT, the mechanistic explanation of the reckoning); SAE-synthesis 2026 (cross-family summary). The verdict: sc-FMs encode cell-type and pathway features cleanly, but not regulatory or causal features. The white-box alternatives: CausCell (Nat Comm 2025, SCM + diffusion → interpretable cellular reps with explicit cDAGs), VC-World (arXiv 2025, white-box LLM-driven simulator with stepwise mechanistic reasoning). The framing pieces: Blueprint-revisit (Nat Reviews 2026) asks what an interpretable virtual cell would even look like; Theory-in-cell-grammar argues for an interpretable grammar layer.
