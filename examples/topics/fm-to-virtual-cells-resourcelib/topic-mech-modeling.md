Mech-modelling is the parallel universe to AIVC — same goal (predict cellular state), different premise (write the equations rather than learn them). PhysiCell (2018) is the agent-based multicellular simulator and the only truly mechanistic approach to virtual tumours; CompuCell3D is the community's annual gathering. The bridge to AIVC: Nat Biotech minimal-life (2026) is the framing piece on minimal-cell simulation as the AIVC counterpoint. UIUC 4DWCM is the explicit mech-modelling-meets-AIVC bridge — a 4D whole-cell model that uses learned components. Theory-in-cell-grammar argues for an interpretable grammar layer so virtual-cell labs can democratise. COMPASS is a concept-bottleneck FM for immunotherapy response, beating 22 baselines with interpretable cell-type-level features — mechanistic spirit inside an FM. Noetik-Octo-VC (1.5B params, GSK partnership) is the commercial-scale instantiation.
