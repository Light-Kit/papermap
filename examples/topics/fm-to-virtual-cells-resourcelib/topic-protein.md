Protein FMs are the family with the cleanest scale curve and the most peer-reviewed wins. Structure: AlphaFold2 (2021, the breakthrough), AlphaFold3 (2024, full complexes). Sequence representation: ESM-2 (2023, open, the academic workhorse, UCE's cross-species bridge), ESM-3 (2025, 98B params, multimodal, generated esmGFP de novo, cleanest compute disclosure). Generative design: RFDiffusion (2023, diffusion exemplar), ProGen2 (2023, generative sequence pre-ESM3), Proteina/Complexa (63.5 % wet-lab hit on PDGFR binders). Benchmarks: ProteinGym (variant effect), CASP/CAPRI (community structure + complex assessments). Interpretability — the new front: Adams 2025 is the protein-FM SAE paper that started the biology-FM interpretability wave; Hunklinger & Ferruz 2026 is the review. Compute landscape: DeepMind/Isomorphic Labs is the TPU-scale closed-weights commercial arm.
