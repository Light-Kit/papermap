The drug-design thread sits at the intersection of perturbation prediction, protein design, and pharma commercialisation. AlphaFold3 (2024) extended structure prediction to full complexes (proteins + nucleic acids + ions + ligands). RFDiffusion (2023) is the diffusion-based de novo design exemplar. New 2026 entries: MAP-Perturb (zero-shot for unprofiled drugs), sc-FM-perturb-adapter (<1 % trainable params on frozen FM, generalises where CPA/GEARS/scGen don't), PerturbFate (convergent regulators of melanoma drug resistance), AIvEVs (EV-focused AIVC). DrugReflector (2026) and UNAGI (Nat BME 2025, time-series scRNA virtual-disease simulator) anchor the in-silico screening side. TxGNN (2024) bridges gene-level and patient-level. DrugCLIP is the zero-shot virtual-screen baseline. Commercial signal: Turbine $25M raise; JPM 2026 Lilly–Nvidia is the largest pharma–AI infrastructure deal.
