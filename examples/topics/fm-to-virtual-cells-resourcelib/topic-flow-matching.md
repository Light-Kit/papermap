Flow-matching is a single-item topic but a load-bearing technique. The Morehead 2026 review ("flow matching, the generative technique under generative virtual cells") names it as the substrate the post-reckoning generative VC wave is building on. Flow-matching learns a deterministic velocity field that transports a base distribution to a target distribution — comparable to diffusion in expressiveness, often cheaper to sample, and with cleaner conditioning behaviour. The reason this matters for virtual cells: cellular state is naturally a distribution (over genes, over cells, over perturbation conditions), and flow-matching gives a principled way to map a baseline distribution into a perturbed one. Adjacent to the diffusion and generative topics. Expect this single item to multiply quickly as the architectural response wave matures — most 2026–2027 generative VC papers will use either diffusion or flow-matching as the backbone.
