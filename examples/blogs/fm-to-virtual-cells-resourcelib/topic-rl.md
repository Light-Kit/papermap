RL is a single-item topic but a notable entry-point. CellFluxRL is the 2026 RL-driven virtual-cell paper from the Stanford / CZI / Lundberg axis — biology-FM training with biological constraints encoded as RL rewards. The motivation: if pre-training objectives like next-gene-prediction encode the wrong thing (correlation rather than causality, per the reckoning analysis), then an RL stage that rewards correct counterfactual prediction on held-out perturbations may be the cheapest way to bolt causal pressure onto an existing FM. Adjacent to the causal, perturbation-prediction, and agents topics — the RL framing is shared with the agentic-FM intersection. A topic to watch: 2026 was the year RL re-entered biology AI seriously after AlphaFold's RL-light supremacy. Expect more items as labs port RLHF / RLAIF patterns from LLM research onto biology FMs.
