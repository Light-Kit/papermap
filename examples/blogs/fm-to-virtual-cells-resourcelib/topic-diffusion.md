Diffusion is the generative technique cropping up across the response wave. X-Cell (Wang 2026) is a diffusion LM for perturbation — direct rival to State/scGPT-perturb framing. CausCell (Nat Comm 2025) couples structural causal models with diffusion → interpretable cellular reps with explicit cDAGs, the white-box counter-position to black-box scLLMs. RFDiffusion (2023) is the original biology-side exemplar: diffusion-based de novo protein design. scDiffusion brings diffusion to single-cell expression generation. The pattern: diffusion is now the default generative backbone when the goal is to produce distributions of cellular outcomes (perturbation responses, sequence designs) rather than point estimates.
