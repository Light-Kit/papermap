Genomic FMs have a clean lineage and a 2025 inflection. Pre-2021 anchors: Enformer (gene-expression-from-sequence, the pre-FM-era model). 2021 BERT era: DNABERT-v1. Successors and alternatives: DNABERT-2 (BPE-tokenised, efficiency-focused), Nucleotide Transformer (InstaDeep, multi-genome). The state-space turn: HyenaDNA (non-transformer, single-nucleotide resolution, very long context), Caduceus (bidirectional, reverse-complement-equivariant). 2024–2025 frontier: Evo-v1 (first genomic FM with demonstrated in-context learning), Evo2 (Evo-v1 successor — 1M-token context, ~$5M training, still the only genomic FM with ICL). The 2025 SOTA: AlphaGenome — replaces Enformer + Borzoi, 25/26 wins at 1-Mb context, the new variant-effect ceiling. Consens-genome-LM (2025) is the architectural review. Arc Institute (Evo2, STATE, Tahoe-100M producer) is the largest non-DeepMind compute (~2,048 × H100).
