MERCI (Cell Metabolism) is the computational method spotting cells with mitochondrial-transfer signatures — used as motivation in the AIVM commentary. The bet: if regulatory logic is what current sc-FMs miss (per the SAE wave), then encoding signaling pathways and metabolic state as first-class objects is the architectural fix.
