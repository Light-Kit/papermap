The spatial thread couples cellular state with tissue geometry. Nicheformer (2025) is the spatial-omics sc-FM with a niche-aware objective. mStar (2025) aggregates ST + WSI signals; OmniCell-Spatial and SpatialFusion go further on multimodal integration. CLM-X (2026) adds spatial + temporal context to cell language modelling. Hex-Nat Med (H&E → 40-protein spatial proteomics, +22 % prognostic on 2298 NSCLC patients) is the clinical headline. STAMP-Cell turns Xenium/CosMx/MERSCOPE/PhenoCycler into scalable single-cell profilers — the wet-lab counterpart. Cao virtual-embryos (2026) is the developmental extension: integrating single-cell and spatial data into a simulator. Datasets: HuBMAP (human tissue / spatial atlas), HEST-1k (histology with spatial transcriptomics). Commercial signal: Noetik (spatial-omics AIVC startup) lands GSK.
