Digital-twin is the clinical-translation framing of virtual cells — push the abstraction up to whole organs or whole patients. Zhou virtual-lungs (2026) is the explicit virtual-organ paper: personalised organ-scale simulation on real lungs. Aging-clocks (Cell 2026) is the population-scale instantiation: 2019 individuals → core-capacity / multimodal / organ-specific aging clocks, MAE 3.87y on chronological age. COMPASS (concept-bottleneck FM for immunotherapy response) brings the digital-twin spirit inside an FM: 22-baseline beat with interpretable cell-type-level features. Noetik-Octo-VC (1.5B params, GSK partnership) is the commercial-scale virtual cell targeting clinical oncology at population scale. PhysiCell (2018) is the mechanistic backbone the digital-twin agenda repeatedly references. IBS Park Jongeun is the Korean national-lab investment in human-digital-twin programmes around single-cell data — the geographic third pole after US/EU.
