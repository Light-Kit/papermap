The multimodal thread is mid-maturation. PathChat-2 (instruction-tuned vision-language pathology) and PathChat-DX (FDA Breakthrough) are the production exemplars. ESM-3 (98B params, esmGFP generated de novo) is the multimodal protein flagship. mStar and OmniCell-Spatial fuse spatial-transcriptomics with imaging; SpatialFusion goes further. Hex-Nat Med couples H&E with spatial proteomics; aging-clocks couples genomic, clinical, and behavioural signals. The methodological question came into focus in 2026: SIS (Synergistic Information Score, Microsoft) measures whether the model extracts genuinely cross-modal information rather than just averaging modalities. "Beyond alignment, synergistic" argues multimodal cell-FMs need synergistic (not just aligned) integration. Rao 2026 is the position paper unifying the five FM families under one multimodal frame. AIVM (Trends Biochem) names AI Virtual Metabolism as a coherent metabolism-flavoured agenda.
